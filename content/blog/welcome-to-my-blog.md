## Hello and Welcome!

I built this website as I wanted to start putting my photos out there, rather than have them languish in the cloud. I stopped taking photos with my old camera for several years, and decided in December 2025 to start again with a new camera. 

I was also really frustrated with my workflow and wanted to improve how I could more easily share photos with people - I will share more about that in a future post, and how this site came about with the help of GitHub Copilot and just a single prompt. I've iterated on it since then, but pretty happy with it right now.

### What to Expect

- **Behind the scenes** — The stories and experiences behind my favourite photos
- **Technical Learning** — Learning new techniques with my camera and with my software
- **Gear talk** — Reviews and thoughts on cameras and lenses I use
- **Photo walks** — Recaps from my photography adventures
